Who We Are

Delivering Trust in Healthcare for Over 50 Years

For more than five decades, Khandelwal Distributors has been a trusted name in pharmaceutical distribution, serving hospitals, healthcare institutions, retail pharmacies, and healthcare professionals with integrity, reliability, and excellence.

Our Story

A Family-Led Enterprise, Grown Into a Dependable Partner

Established with a vision to make quality medicines readily available, we have grown from a family-led enterprise into one of Central India's dependable pharmaceutical distribution partners. Our journey has been built on strong relationships, ethical business practices, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Today, we proudly serve 200+ hospitals, healthcare institutions, and retail pharmacy partners, ensuring uninterrupted access to a comprehensive range of pharmaceutical products from India's leading pharmaceutical companies.

Our reputation has been earned through timely deliveries, transparent business practices, competitive pricing, and personalized customer support. We understand that in healthcare, every delivery matters, which is why our team works with precision and dedication to maintain an efficient supply chain.
